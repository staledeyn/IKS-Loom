The Extraction of Zinc
In the ancient technical text Rasaratna Samuccaya, the author Vagbhata describes the
complex process of extracting Zinc, which is known as Yashada. This specific extraction process
is called Kharpara Satvapatana. To execute this process, practitioners utilized a specialized
distillation apparatus called the Tiryak Patana Yantra. To obtain pure zinc, the raw zinc ore
(Kharpara) is first mixed with reducing agents and heated in a closed crucible. The resulting
vapor is condensed in the apparatus. This text proves that the distillation of zinc was mastered
early on.
